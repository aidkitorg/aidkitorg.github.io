Left Behind Workers Fund

Direct Cash Assistance Grant

Applicant Agreement

The Left Behind Workers Fund (LBWF) is hosted at Impact Charitable which is a non-profit organization (5013c). The Fund provides cash assistance grants to people that live in Colorado that do not qualify for other federal relief programs. Confidentiality Protection: LBWF will not share any personal identifiable information with government agencies. There is no risk of public charge by applying for this grant.

As an applicant of the LBWF Direct Cash Assistance program, I understand my expectations to be as follows:

Applicant Attestations: I attest that the following statements are true:

  • The information and any documentation I shall supply in the grant screening process will be accurate, true and complete
  • I am 18 years or older
  • I am NOT eligible for unemployment insurance/benefits
  • I am NOT eligible for the Federal CAREs Act stimulus check
  • I am in need of cash assistance

I have read and understand the information on this contract.
